About Our Practice

Dedicated to Excellence in Oral Surgery

For over two decades, Oral Surgeons of Indiana has been a trusted leader in advanced oral and maxillofacial surgery. Our practice is built on the foundation of clinical excellence, patient safety, and compassionate care.

Our experienced surgeons specialize in a wide range of procedures, from routine wisdom teeth extractions to complex bone grafting and dental implant placement. We utilize the latest surgical techniques and technology to ensure optimal outcomes for every patient.

We understand that oral surgery can be intimidating, which is why we've created a warm, welcoming environment where patients feel comfortable and cared for. Our entire team is committed to making your experience as smooth and painless as possible.
